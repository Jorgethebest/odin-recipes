Chicken wings

Wings a favorite food. Endless way to serve them. Wings are deep fried with some type of seasoning before and after. Usually can be cooked with a flour mixture. If not then plain. Served with a side of ranch wings to add extra flavor

Ingredients

    Chicken
    Oil
    Some type of seasoning
    Flour and egg mixture

Steps

  • Dry wings
  • Add into flour and egg mixture(to add crunch)
  • Have oil of stove and add/li>
  • Cook till golden
  • Add seasoning
  • Add sauce (like hot sauce)
  • Enjoy!
