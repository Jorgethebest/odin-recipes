Tacos

Tacos some of the most beloved food in mexican culture. Although originating from Mexico, they are now enjoyed globally. Being one of the most diverse foods. It is impossible to get tired of them. A simple food consisting of a tortilla and meat. From different types of meat to toppings. Some being Asada(the most common) Cabeza(head), Tripas(guts) and so on.
